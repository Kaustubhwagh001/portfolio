About

I’m a Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) with a passion for designing scalable, observable, and self-healing infrastructure across hybrid and cloud environments. My core expertise spans observability, AIOps, automation, and infrastructure reliability engineering — helping organizations achieve end-to-end visibility and operational excellence. I build and manage centralized observability platforms using Grafana, Prometheus, Loki, Tempo, and Mimir, providing deep insights into distributed systems and microservices. I architect data-driven dashboards, SLO/SLI frameworks, and proactive alerting systems to monitor performance, availability, and latency across multiple applications and services. Leveraging AIOps principles, I design and implement automated anomaly detection, capacity forecasting, and trend analysis pipelines for large-scale server and service environments. This enables predictive scaling, early incident detection, and intelligent decision-making for performance optimization and cost management. I drive Infrastructure as Code (IaC) and automation initiatives using Terraform, Ansible, and CI/CD pipelines (Azure DevOps, Cloud Build, GitOps) to ensure consistency, repeatability, and fast deployments across environments. These practices have significantly reduced manual intervention and operational risk in production. As part of resilience and disaster recovery planning, I have implemented RTO (Recovery Time Objective) and RPO (Recovery Point Objective)-based backup and failover strategies for critical databases and applications, ensuring business continuity even under large-scale failures. My focus is on creating adaptive, autonomous, and intelligent infrastructure — where telemetry, automation, and AIOps-driven insights work together to ensure high availability, optimized resource utilization, and faster incident resolution at scale.
